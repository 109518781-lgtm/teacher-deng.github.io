课文核心句型 · Lesson 83-84

📖 现在完成时经典对话
A: Have you had your lunch yet?
B: Yes, I have already had it.
A: Have you ever been to Paris?
B: No, I have never been there.
A: I've already packed my suitcase.
B: He has gone to London. (他去了伦敦，尚未回来)
A: She has been to New York twice. (她去过纽约两次，已回来)

🔑 语法聚焦：现在完成时 (have/has + 过去分词) / already/yet用法 / have been to vs have gone to
✨ 口诀： “been to去过回，gone to去了未归；already肯定句，yet问否随其后。”
📊 have been to vs have gone to 核心辨析
• have been to：曾经去过（已返回），常与 ever/never/twice 连用
• have gone to：去了某地（人未返回），不与次数连用

词汇 & 短语总结

📌 核心词汇（含第二大题）
mess凌乱 pack打包 suitcase手提箱 leave离开 already已经 yet还，仍然 holiday假期 enjoy享受 ready准备好的 lunch午餐
🔗 高频短语
• have a holiday 度假   • pack one's suitcase 整理手提箱
• leave for + 地点 动身前往   • enjoy oneself 玩得开心
• be in a mess 杂乱无章   • What a mess! 真乱啊！
• be ready for 为...做好准备   • at the moment 此刻
