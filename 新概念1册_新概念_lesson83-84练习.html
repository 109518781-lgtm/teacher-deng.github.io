课文原文 · Lesson 83 Going on holiday

📖 Going on holiday (度假)

CAROL: Hello, Sam. Come in.
SAM: Hi, Sam. We're having lunch. Do you want to have lunch with us?
TOM: No, thank you, Tom. I've already had lunch. I had lunch at half past twelve.
SAM: Have a cup of coffee then.
TOM: I've just had a cup, thank you. I had one after my lunch.
CAROL: Let's go into the living room, Carol. We can have our coffee there.
SAM: Excuse the mess, Sam. This room's very untidy. We're packing our suitcases. We're going to leave tomorrow. Tom and I are going to have a holiday.
TOM: Aren't you lucky!
CAROL: When are you going to have a holiday, Sam?
SAM: I don't know. I've already had my holiday this year.
TOM: Where did you go?
SAM: I stayed at home!
✨ 现在完成时口诀： 已完成，影响现在；already肯定句，yet问与否；have/has+过分，时间标志需记熟。
📌 语法对比： I've already had lunch (已完成，对现在的影响：不饿) vs I had lunch at half past twelve (只说明过去时间)
