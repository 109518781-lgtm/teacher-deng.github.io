课文原文 · Lesson 79-80

📖 Shopping List (购物清单)
Carol: What are you doing, Tom?
Tom: I'm making a shopping list.
Carol: What do we need?
Tom: We need a lot of things. We need some vegetables, fruit, milk and bread.
Carol: And we haven't got any tea or coffee.
Tom: I'll add them to the list.

🔑 语法聚焦：现在进行时 / 情态动词 need / 可数不可数名词 / 一般将来时 will
