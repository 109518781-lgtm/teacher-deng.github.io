课文原文 · Lesson 65-66

📖 Not a baby (不是一个孩子)
Father: What are you going to do this evening, Jill?
Jill: I'm going to meet some friends, Dad.
Father: You mustn't come home late. You must be home at half past ten.
Jill: I can't get home so early, Dad! I can get home at a quarter to eleven.
Father: Very well. Have a good time.
Jill: We always enjoy ourselves, Dad.
Father: Enjoy yourselves!

🔑 语法聚焦：反身代词 (myself/yourself/ourselves等) / 时间表达 (half past, quarter to) / must用法
✨ 反身代词口诀： 一二称用形代后加self，三称人称代宾加self；单数self复数selves，动作回射记心间。
