课文原文 · Lesson 83-84

📖 Going on holiday (度假)
CAROL: Hello, Sam. Come in.
SAM: Hi, Carol. Where's Tom?
CAROL: He's upstairs. He's having a bath.
SAM: Tom!
TOM: Yes?
SAM: I'm having breakfast.
TOM: What are you doing?
SAM: I'm having breakfast.
TOM: Have you had it yet?
SAM: Yes, I've already had it.
TOM: What are you going to do now?
SAM: I'm going to have a bath.
TOM: Have you had one yet?
SAM: No, I haven't.

🔑 语法聚焦：现在完成时 (have/has + 过去分词) / already/yet用法 / have的各种短语
✨ 现在完成时口诀： 已完成，影响现在；already肯定句，yet问与否；have/has+过分，时间标志需记熟。
